Our Story

More Than
Martial Arts

Nayak Budokan Karate Academy is dedicated to shaping strong individuals through authentic traditional karate training. We believe karate is not merely a physical practice — it is a way of life that builds character, focus, and inner strength.

Our mission is to instil discipline, confidence, self-respect, and moral values in students of all ages — children, youth, and adults alike — transforming lives one kata at a time.

Karate begins with respect and ends with character.

— NBKA Founding Philosophy

Meet the Sensei

The Master
Behind NBKA

Sensei P. Ganesh Nayak is the Founder and Chief Instructor of NBKA — a practitioner of profound discipline, technique, and spirit.

Trained directly under Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao, he carries the authentic Budokan tradition forward with unwavering dedication.

  • Direct lineage — trained under Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao
  • Certified Budokan Karate-Do instructor
  • Decades of teaching experience in Hyderabad
  • Students competing at state & national tournaments
