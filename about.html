About Nayak Budokan Karate Academy

About Nayak Budokan Karate Academy (NBKA)

Nayak Budokan Karate Academy (NBKA) was founded by the visionary P. Ganesh Nayak along with his dedicated team, under the divine guidance, blessings, and mentorship of Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao. With an unwavering passion for martial arts and a deep commitment to empowering individuals, NBKA was established as a center of discipline, self-defense, and character development.

The name Nayak Budokan Karate Academy was graciously bestowed by Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao as a mark of love, respect, and recognition for his beloved student, P. Ganesh Nayak. This sacred teacher–student bond reflects the core values of martial arts — trust, loyalty, humility, and lifelong learning.

Our Lineage and Budokan Legacy

Nayak Budokan Karate Academy is proudly affiliated with Budokan Karate-Do International, a globally respected martial arts organization.

Budokan Karate-Do International was founded in 1966 by Grand Master Chew Choo Soot in Malaysia. His vision blended traditional martial arts values with modern techniques, creating a comprehensive path for physical and mental excellence.

Budokan Karate-Do was introduced to India in 1978 by Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao, who was personally mentored by Grand Master Chew Choo Soot.

Vision, Mission, and Social Responsibility

NBKA’s mission goes beyond teaching karate techniques. It is a commitment to empower individuals — especially children and women — with confidence, courage, and self-belief.

Many students trained under P. Ganesh Nayak have achieved success at state, national, and international tournaments, reflecting both technical excellence and disciplined training.

Karate for All – A Way of Life

Nayak Budokan Karate Academy is not just a training center — it is a family. Students of all age groups come together to learn discipline, confidence, and inner strength through karate.

Honoring Our Grand Master

NBKA stands as a living tribute to Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao, whose teachings and values continue to guide every student and instructor.
