Sensei

Founder & Chief Instructor

P. Ganesh Nayak is the Founder and Chief Instructor of Nayak Budokan Karate Academy. He established NBKA with the vision of spreading authentic karate and strong moral values among students.

Trained under the guidance of Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao, Sensei Ganesh Nayak follows traditional Budokan values of discipline, humility, and character before combat.
