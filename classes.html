Our Classes

Kids Karate Program

Our Kids Karate Program is designed to build discipline, confidence, coordination, and respect from an early age. Children learn basic karate techniques in a safe, encouraging, and structured environment.

Teen & Adult Training

This program focuses on physical fitness, self-defense, and mental strength. Students develop flexibility, stamina, and focus while learning traditional karate techniques and forms.

Advanced & Black Belt Training

Advanced training is meant for dedicated students who wish to master higher-level techniques, kata, sparring, and self-discipline. Emphasis is placed on leadership, control, and perfection of form.

Class Timings

Class timings are scheduled to suit students of different age groups. Please contact the academy directly for the latest batch timings and enrollment details.
