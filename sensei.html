Sensei

Founder & Chief Instructor

Sensei P. Ganesh Nayak is the founder and chief instructor of Nayak Budokan Karate Academy. With years of dedicated practice and disciplined training, he has devoted his life to spreading the true spirit of karate.

Trained under the guidance and blessings of Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao, Sensei Ganesh Nayak believes that karate is not just about physical strength, but about character, humility, and self-control.

Under his leadership, students of NBKA have grown into confident, disciplined individuals and have participated in various state, national, and international tournaments.
