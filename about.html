About NBKA

Our Mission

Nayak Budokan Karate Academy is dedicated to shaping strong individuals through traditional karate training. Our mission is to instill discipline, confidence, self-respect, and moral values in students of all ages.

We believe karate is not just a physical practice, but a way of life that builds character, focus, and inner strength before combat.

Our Lineage & Legacy

Nayak Budokan Karate Academy follows the authentic Budokan Karate-Do system, founded in 1966 by Grand Master Chew Choo Soot.

Budokan Karate-Do was introduced in India in 1978 by Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao, under whose guidance and blessings our academy proudly continues the Budokan tradition.

NBKA stands as a tribute to this rich lineage, preserving the values of respect, discipline, and lifelong learning.
