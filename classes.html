Training Programs

Little Warriors (Kids Program)

Age Group: 5 to 12 years

The Little Warriors program is specially designed to introduce children to the fundamentals of karate in a safe, disciplined, and encouraging environment.

Through structured training, children develop focus, respect, coordination, physical fitness, and self-confidence. The program also helps in improving listening skills, behavior, and overall discipline.

Our instructors ensure that every child learns the values of karate such as respect for elders, self-control, and perseverance, while enjoying the training process.

Warrior Path (Teens & Adults)

Age Group: 13 years and above

The Warrior Path program is designed for teenagers and adults who wish to improve physical fitness, mental strength, and self-defense skills through traditional karate training.

This program focuses on building stamina, flexibility, balance, and coordination, while also helping students manage stress, improve concentration, and develop self-confidence.

Training sessions include basic to intermediate karate techniques, conditioning exercises, and practical self-defense applications suitable for real-life situations.

Black Belt Track (Advanced Training)

Eligibility: Intermediate and advanced students

The Black Belt Track is designed for committed students who wish to deepen their understanding of karate and pursue excellence in both technique and character.

This program focuses on advanced kata, kumite, physical conditioning, weapons training, and preparation for tournaments at state, national, and international levels.

Students on this path are guided to develop leadership qualities, discipline, and a strong sense of responsibility, preparing them not only for higher belt ranks but also to become role models within the dojo.
