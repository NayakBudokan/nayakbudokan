Founder & Chief Instructor

P. Ganesh Nayak

Founder – Nayak Budokan Karate Academy

P. Ganesh Nayak is a renowned Karate expert and the founder of Nayak Budokan Karate Academy. With a deep passion for martial arts, he established the academy in 2009 under the guidance of Grand Master C. Hanumantha Rao.

Affiliated with Budokan Karate-Do International, Nayak Budokan Karate Academy has grown into a respected training center for Karate enthusiasts. As the founder and chief instructor, P. Ganesh Nayak has dedicated himself to spreading the true spirit of Karate and mentoring the next generation of martial artists.

Under his leadership, the academy has produced numerous skilled practitioners who have achieved success in state, national, and international competitions.
