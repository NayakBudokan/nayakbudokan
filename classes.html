Training Programs

Little Warriors (Kids Program)

Age Group: 5 to 12 years

The Little Warriors program is specially designed to introduce children to the fundamentals of karate in a safe, disciplined, and encouraging environment.

Through structured training, children develop focus, respect, coordination, physical fitness, and self-confidence. The program also helps in improving listening skills, behavior, and overall discipline.

Our instructors ensure that every child learns the values of karate such as respect for elders, self-control, and perseverance, while enjoying the training process.
