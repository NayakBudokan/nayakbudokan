About Nayak Budokan Karate Academy

About NBKA

Nayak Budokan Karate Academy (NBKA) was founded by the visionary P. Ganesh Nayak, under the guidance and mentorship of Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao. NBKA stands as a center of discipline, self-defense, and character development.

The name Nayak Budokan Karate Academy reflects a sacred teacher–student bond, symbolizing trust, loyalty, humility, and lifelong learning.

Our Lineage and Legacy

NBKA is proudly affiliated with Budokan Karate-Do International, founded in 1966 by Grand Master Chew Choo Soot.

Budokan Karate-Do was introduced to India in 1978 by Grand Master C. Hanumanth Rao.

Karate – A Way of Life

NBKA is more than a training center — it is a family that nurtures confidence, discipline, and inner strength in students of all ages.
